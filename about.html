about

Hey! The name's Kim (she/her) but I also go by Dashi (short for Pandashi).

Throughout school, I slowly came to realize that art was what I truly wanted to do. So I threw all my mechatronics engineering plans out the window (albiet still finishing physics?) to continue creating. I currently work as the Illustrator for Warriors eSports at the University of Waterloo (I study design there, too!). I also work as a freelance illustrator and concept artist.

I'm just a girl who constantly immerses herself into the fictional worlds of games, shows, and books. In turn, art, storytelling, and content creation have become immense passions of mine. Now, I strive to break into the gaming industry. I hope to work as a concept artist and create games for many to enjoy. My ultimate goal as an artist is to create what matters...especially to me.

Skills: Clip Studio Paint, Procreate, Photoshop, Illustrator, Live 2D rigging, traditional art, can do physics, sense of humour (?), trash aim in any FPS
